Le Cagibi

Café by day, venue by night. Intimate space with a policy of anti-oppression.
Open to all types of events as well as music performance- endeavouring to focus on quality and social conscience. Priority given to music and events we think will work well in, and respect the space.
Capacity: 70 standing (40 if seated)
Booking Price: 75$ booking fee. Some exceptions are made for events focused around social justice work.
Sound Engineer Available: Yes
Website: Le Cagibi
5490 saint Laurent, Montreal, QC, CA
