We connect musicians and venues in Montreal

Whether you manage a bar or a band, ConcertConnect takes the hassle out of planning live shows.

Want to book a band?

The days of spending hours looking for contact information are over. We have a list of local bands looking for places to play, along with their genre, ratings from previous gigs, and samples of their latest tunes.

Want to play live music?

Getting a live gig can be tough without a booking agent. We've got Montreal venues who are looking for local talent, so put yourself out there! We'll give you detailed info on each venue, including capacity and high-res stage pics.

Looking for bandmates?

Find the perfect match for your band by browsing our list of musicians who want to join a local group. We've got their musical interests and sound clips, making it easy to find the right fit.
